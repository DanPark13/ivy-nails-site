About Us

Welcome to Ivy Organic Nails & Spa, your sanctuary of natural beauty and relaxation nestled in the heart of Oceanside, Long Island.

Who We Are

At Ivy Organic Nails & Spa, we believe that self-care is not just a luxury but an essential aspect of a balanced and fulfilling life. Our mission is to provide a serene and inviting environment where you can escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and indulge in a rejuvenating experience that nourishes both your body and soul.

As a leading organic nails and spa destination, we take pride in our commitment to using only the highest quality, non-toxic, and eco-friendly products. Your well-being is our top priority, and we believe in pampering you with treatments that not only enhance your natural beauty but also promote overall wellness.

Our team of skilled and passionate professionals is dedicated to crafting personalized experiences that cater to your unique needs and preferences. Whether you're looking for a flawless manicure, a refreshing pedicure, or a therapeutic massage, we have a range of services designed to cater to all your desires.

Step into our elegant and contemporary space, where tranquility meets sophistication. Each detail has been carefully curated to ensure a soothing ambiance, providing you with a peaceful escape from the outside world. From the moment you walk through our doors, you'll be greeted with warmth and genuine care by our friendly staff, who are always ready to assist you in any way they can.

We strive to stay ahead of the latest trends and techniques in the industry, continuously refining our skills and knowledge to deliver exceptional results. Whether you're preparing for a special occasion or simply seeking a moment of serenity, our team is here to make your experience unforgettable.

At Ivy Organic Nails & Spa, we are more than just a beauty destination; we are a community built on trust, excellence, and compassion. We cherish the relationships we forge with our clients and value each one as a part of our extended family.

Join us on this journey towards holistic well-being and rejuvenation. Experience the magic of Ivy Organic Nails & Spa in Oceanside, Long Island, and allow us to redefine the way you think about self-care.
