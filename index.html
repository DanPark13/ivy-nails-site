Intro

Located in the tranquil heart of Oceanside, NY, just a breezy 10-minute drive from the sun-kissed shores of Long Beach, our nail salon is a haven of natural elegance and purity.

As a dedicated natural nail lounge, we embrace the ethos of beauty in harmony with nature. Our commitment to your well-being is reflected in our exclusive use of non-toxic, non-GMO products, ensuring a safe and eco-friendly experience.

We uphold the highest standards of hygiene, employing hospital-grade cleaning procedures for all our tools, ensuring a pristine and worry-free environment for you.

At our salon, elegance is not just a standard; it's our nature. We invite you to experience the fusion of luxury and natural beauty, where each treatment is a testament to our dedication to excellence and environmental consciousness.
